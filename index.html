Kuansheng Chen

I am an undergraduate in Computer Science at National Chiao Tung University (NCTU) in Taiwan. I'm fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yen-Yu Lin, and have collaborated with Dr. Chien-Yao Wang and Prof. Mark Liao from Academia Sinica.

My research interests lie within object detection and object tracking. I am currently working on the problem of Open World Object Detection (OWOD). My goal is to build a detector that can correctly detect previous unknown objects and improve its ability in the incremental learning fashion.
