I am an undergraduate in Computer Science at National Chiao Tung University (NCTU) in Taiwan. I'm fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yen-Yu Lin and have collaborated closely with Dr. Chien-Yao Wang and Prof. Mark Liao from Academia Sinica.
